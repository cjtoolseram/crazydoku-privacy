Privacy Policy for Crazydoku

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

Introduction

Crazydoku ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Crazydoku (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

Advertising Data (via Google AdMob):

When you use our App, Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized advertisements, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • Location data (general location, not precise)
  • IP address

This information is collected by Google AdMob, not directly by us. For more information about how Google uses this data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account information (no login required)
  • Game data (all game data is stored locally on your device)
  • Statistics (all statistics are stored locally on your device)
  • Any data that can personally identify you

How We Use Information

Local Storage

  • All game data, statistics, and history are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not have access to your game data
  • Your data never leaves your device (except for ad requests handled by Google AdMob)

Advertising

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App
  • AdMob uses the information described above to show you relevant ads
  • We do not control how Google AdMob uses this information
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: As described above, Google AdMob collects advertising data. This is handled by Google, not by us.

Data Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage
  • We do not transmit your game data to any servers
  • Your privacy is protected by Android's built-in security features

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to your device Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Delete app data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Access your data: All your data is stored locally on your device

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Privacy Policy for Samadhi

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Samadhi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Samadhi (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App has no accounts, no login, no analytics, and no advertising.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account information (no login required)
  • Meditation session data (all data is stored locally on your device)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data that can personally identify you

How Your Data Is Stored

  • All meditation sessions, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently

Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications: Used only to deliver daily meditation reminders you configure in Settings. No notification data is sent to us.
  • Vibration: Used for optional haptic feedback during sessions. No data is collected.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No data is shared with any third party.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone. There is no data to share.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any user, no information from children under 13 is collected either.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access your data: All data is on your device, in the app
  • Delete your data: Clear app data in Android Settings, or uninstall the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

Privacy Policy for LessonOS

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

LessonOS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application LessonOS (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any information.

LessonOS is a fully offline application. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account information (no login required)
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Any data that can personally identify you

How Your Data Is Stored

  • All data (students, classes, attendance, leave requests) is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required to use the App

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. For information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties. The App does not connect to any external servers or services (other than Google Play for in-app purchases).

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage
  • We do not transmit your data to any servers
  • Your privacy is protected by Android's built-in security features

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete app data: Use the reset function in the App's settings, or uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Access your data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible within the App

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use the 2048 Puzzle Offline mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information Collection and Use

The Service is designed to be an offline-first application. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal contact information to use the game logic.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

The Service uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized advertising and analytics. This may include:

  • IP address (which may be used to estimate general location)
  • Advertising IDs (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • App performance data and debugging information

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your Android device settings.

3. Data Storage

All game progress, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
